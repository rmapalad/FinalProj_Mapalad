About Lucban

The municipality is known for its annual Pahiyas Festival, which is held every May 15 in honor of San Isidro Labrador and known also as the 'Baguio' counterpart of the province.

About PAHIYAS

The Pahiyas Festival is a vibrant celebration of harvest and thanksgiving held annually in Lucban, Quezon, Philippines.

A Harvest Festivale

Pahiyas is a celebration of a bountiful harvest, honoring the patron saint of farmers, San Isidro Labrador.

A Visual Feast

Houses are adorned with intricate decorations made from fruits, vegetables, and rice wafers, creating a stunning display of colors and textures.

A Cultural Extravaganza

The festival features various cultural performances, parades, and contests, showcasing the town's rich traditions and artistry.
